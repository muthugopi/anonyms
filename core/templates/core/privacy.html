Trust & Safety

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Anonyms. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

2. Information We Collect

During registration and verification, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Department
  • Academic year
  • Profile information
  • Any information voluntarily submitted by users

We also collect technical information such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Login activity

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Verify student accounts
  • Provide access to the platform
  • Maintain account security
  • Prevent spam, abuse, and fraudulent activity
  • Improve platform functionality

4. Anonymous Posts

Posts published through Anonyms are displayed anonymously to other users. While administrators may have access to certain account information for moderation, user identities will not be publicly displayed alongside anonymous content.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties. Information may only be disclosed:

  • When required by law
  • To investigate violations of platform rules
  • To protect the safety and security of users

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no online system can guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain user information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Maintain user accounts
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce platform policies

Deleted accounts may have certain records retained for security, legal, or audit purposes.

8. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for:

  • Maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials
  • Avoiding the sharing of sensitive personal information in posts
  • Following community guidelines and platform rules

9. Children's Privacy

Anonyms is intended for students and individuals who meet the minimum age requirements applicable under local laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, users may contact the Anonyms administration team through the platform's support or contact page.

Your system usage information is processed metrics-wise safely to keep campus dialogues protective.
