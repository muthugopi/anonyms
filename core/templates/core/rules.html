Platform Governance

Anonyms Community Rules

To maintain a safe, welcoming, and authentic space for the RIT student campus body, all users must adhere to our core standards.

1. Stay Anonymous

Do not reveal your identity or another person's identity. No sharing names, register numbers, phone numbers, email addresses, or social media handles.

2. Respect Others

No harassment, bullying, threats, or hate speech. Disagreements are allowed, personal attacks are not.

3. No False Accusations

Do not post unverified allegations against individuals, faculty, or organizations. Defamatory content will be removed.

4. Protect Privacy

Do not share screenshots of private conversations without consent. No leaking personal information (doxxing).

5. No Illegal or Harmful Content

No violence, self-harm promotion, illegal activities, or dangerous challenges. No content that encourages criminal behavior.

6. No Explicit Adult Content

No pornography or sexually explicit material. Discussions about relationships are allowed if respectful.

7. No Spam

No advertisements, promotions, referral links, or repetitive posts. No fake engagement or bot activity.

8. Keep Content Relevant

Posts should relate to student life, college experiences, confessions, opinions, discussions, or campus-related topics.

9. Moderation Rights

Administrators may remove content that violates rules. Repeated violations may result in account suspension or permanent removal.

10. Report Violations

Users should report inappropriate posts instead of engaging in arguments.

Verification Rules

  • Only verified RIT students may create anonymous posts.
  • Registration information is handled programmatically and used strictly for unique verification validation.
  • User identities remain permanently detached and hidden from public platform spaces and other peers.
  • System moderators are bound to strict technical confidentiality constraints regarding verification registries.
Disclaimer Anonyms is an independent student community platform. Opinions expressed in posts belong solely to the respective authors and do not represent Ramco Institute of Technology (RIT) or the Anonyms administration team. Users remain entirely responsible for the ethical contexts of the content they publish.
