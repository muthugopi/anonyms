How It Works

Getting started is simple and secure.

1 Sign Up

Create an account using your official RIT student email address.

2 Verify Identity

Click the secure link sent to your academic email to verify you are a current student. Your email is only used for verification and is permanently detached from your posts.

3 Join Discussions

Browse various tags like 'Academics', 'Hostel', 'Placements', and 'General' to see what the campus is talking about.

4 Share Anonymously

Create your first thread or leave a comment. Your identity remains hidden behind an anonymous handle.
